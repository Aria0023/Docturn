The solution

Meet DocTurn

One system that automates the ED-to-hospitalist hand-off end to end — so the right patient reaches the right provider in seconds.

Automatic routing
Round-robin assigns each admit to the next eligible hospitalist by lowest census.
Notifications that land
In-app, push, and SMS cascade until the assigned provider responds.
Secure messaging
Direct, group, and broadcast threads — audited, PHI-safe, no personal texting.
