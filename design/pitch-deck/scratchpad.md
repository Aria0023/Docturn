# DocTurn — CEO Pitch Deck (10 slides)

Audience: CEO of a large hospital (economic buyer / executive). NOT clinicians.
Frame everything in executive terms: patient throughput, ED boarding, length of
stay, physician retention, compliance/legal risk, time-to-value, ROI.

Voice: calm, competent colleague. Clear over clever. No alarmism. Sentence case.
Visual system: DocTurn marketing personality — sky→white→rose gradient on
cover/section/close; white slides for content. System sans. Blue/sky accents,
slate neutrals, status colors used sparingly. Lucide icons. No emoji.

## Title sequence (chapters — short topic noun-phrases)
1. Cover — "Patient hand-offs, without the phone tag." (proposal for the hospital)
2. The hand-off problem
3. The cost of a slow hand-off        (BIG NUMBERS — industry benchmarks, labelled)
4. Meet DocTurn                       (what it is — three pillars)
5. How DocTurn works                  (4-step routing diagram)
6. Inside the product                 (assignment-queue mockup, static HTML)
7. The operational impact             (Today vs With DocTurn — comparison)
8. Compliance by design               (HIPAA/SOC2 — de-risk grid)
9. Live in a day                      (implementation timeline + simple pricing)
10. A 60-day pilot                    (the ask / CTA + success metrics)

Read titles only: problem → cost → solution → how → product → impact → risk →
speed → ask. Flows as a clean exec narrative.

## Layout rhythm / variety
- S1 cover: gradient, wordmark, big headline, presenter line
- S2 problem: white, 3 friction points + simple "before" call-chain visual
- S3 cost: white, 3 oversized figures (labelled as industry/illustrative)
- S4 meet: gradient-tinted, one line + 3 pillar cards w/ icons
- S5 how: white, 4 numbered steps horizontal
- S6 product: white, full product mockup (sky frame)
- S7 impact: white, two-column Today vs DocTurn table/contrast
- S8 compliance: white, 6-point grid + HIPAA/SOC2 badges
- S9 live: gradient-tinted, 3-step timeline + pricing line
- S10 pilot: gradient (mirror cover), the ask + 3 success metrics + contact

## Honesty note on numbers
Slide 3 = national/industry context, clearly labelled "Industry benchmarks."
Slide 7 outcome figures labelled as DocTurn product targets / illustrative.
No fabricated named-customer results.
